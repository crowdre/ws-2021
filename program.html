The 5th International Workshop on Crowd-Based Requirements Engineering (CrowdRE'21)



21 September 2021
Virtual Conference
In conjunction with RE 2021

Program

CrowdRE 2021 is intended to be a full-day workshop held on September 21st, 2021. CrowdRE will be co-located with the RE 2021, a digital conference.

The workshop intends to promote lively discussions among participants from both industry and academia.

The paper sessions in the below program include 20-minute slots (10 minutes for the presentation, and 10 minutes for the discussion) for each paper. After each paper presentation, a discussant opens the discussion by putting the paper into a scientific perspective, listing the contributions, and raising issues and questions for discussions.
